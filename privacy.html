Privacy Policy

Your data.
Your device.
Your control.

Samaritam is built with a privacy-first approach. This page explains exactly what happens to any information you provide when using the tool.

Last updated: March 2026

The short version

Samaritam does not sell, share, or misuse your data. The only external services that receive any data are Google's Gemini API (article text for analysis) and Google Firebase (outlet names and averaged scores if you sign in). No article content is ever stored.

What happens to text you paste

Text sent to Google Gemini API

When you paste text and click Analyse, that text is sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis. This is the core function of the tool and cannot work otherwise. Google's API terms of service apply to this transmission.

Not stored by Samaritam

Samaritam does not store, log, or retain the text you paste. Once the analysis is returned, the text exists only in your browser session. Closing the tab removes it entirely.

No human ever reads your text

No member of Team Samaritam can see what you paste. Article text passes through our Cloudflare Worker proxy to reach Gemini — it is never written to any database or log.

Google Sign-In and accounts

Sign-in is optional

You can use Samaritam fully without signing in. Signing in with Google is only required if you want your Sources history backed up across devices.

What we store when you sign in

When you sign in and save a source, we store the outlet name and your averaged bias scores in Google Firebase Firestore under your unique user ID. We store nothing else — no name, no email, no browsing history, no article text.

Global averages

When you save a source, your scores are also added to the global outlet averages visible to all users in the Top 10 leaderboard. These are aggregated numbers only — no individual analysis is ever attributed to you.

Deleting your data

You can remove any outlet from your Sources tracker at any time using the Remove button. To delete your account data entirely, contact us via the Contact Us page.

My Sources tracker — guest users

Stored on your device only

If you don't sign in, outlet data is saved in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device. Clearing your browser data will remove it.

Storage size

A full year of daily analyses would use less than 1MB of localStorage — negligible. It will not noticeably affect your device.

Cookies and tracking

Samaritam uses no advertising cookies, no analytics, and no tracking pixels. Google Firebase uses a session token to maintain your sign-in state — this is strictly functional and contains no personal information beyond your user ID.

Third-party services

Google Gemini API

Used to perform the bias analysis. Text you paste is transmitted to Google's servers. See Google's Gemini API terms.

Google Firebase (Authentication + Firestore)

Used for Google Sign-In and to store outlet scores for signed-in users. Only outlet names and averaged scores are stored. See Firebase's privacy policy.

Google Fonts

Used to load the Playfair Display and DM Sans typefaces. Your IP address may be logged by Google as part of the font request.

GitHub Pages

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log basic access information such as IP addresses. See GitHub's privacy policy.

Cloudflare Workers

API requests are routed through a Cloudflare Worker that acts as a secure proxy. Cloudflare may log basic request metadata. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how Samaritam handles data, reach out via the Contact Us page.
