Privacy Policy

Your data.
Your device.
Your control.

Samaritam is built with a privacy-first approach. This page explains exactly what happens to any information you provide when using the tool.

Last updated: March 2026

The short version

Samaritam does not collect, store, or track any personal information. There are no accounts, no cookies, no analytics, and no servers receiving your data. The only external service that receives any data is Google's Gemini API — and only the text you choose to analyse.

What happens to text you paste

Text sent to Google Gemini API

When you paste text and click Analyse, that text is sent to Google's Gemini API for analysis. This is the core function of the tool and cannot work otherwise. Google's API terms of service apply to this transmission.

Not stored by Samaritam

Samaritam does not store, log, or retain the text you paste. Once the analysis is returned, the text exists only in your browser session. Closing the tab removes it.

No human ever reads your text

No member of Team Samaritam can see what you paste. There is no backend server, no database, and no logging infrastructure on our end.

My Sources tracker

Stored on your device only

When you save a news outlet to your Sources tracker, that data is stored in your browser's localStorage — a small storage area on your own device. It never leaves your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Persists across sessions

Closing your browser or restarting your device does not delete your Sources data. It persists until you manually clear it using the Remove button in My Sources, or until you clear your browser's site data.

Storage size

A full year of daily analyses would use less than 1MB of localStorage. This is negligible — browsers typically allocate 5–10MB per site. It will not noticeably affect your device.

Cookies and tracking

Samaritam uses no cookies, no analytics, no tracking pixels, and no third-party scripts other than Google Fonts (for typography). We do not know who you are, where you are, or what you have analysed.

Third-party services

Google Gemini API

Used to perform the bias analysis. Text you paste is transmitted to Google's servers. See Google's Gemini API terms.

Google Fonts

Used to load the Playfair Display and DM Sans typefaces. Your IP address may be logged by Google as part of the font request. No personal data is shared.

GitHub Pages

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may log basic access information such as IP addresses as part of standard server operation. See GitHub's privacy policy.

Cloudflare Workers

API requests are routed through a Cloudflare Worker that acts as a secure proxy. Cloudflare may log basic request metadata. See Cloudflare's privacy policy.

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how Samaritam handles data, reach out via the Contact Us page.
